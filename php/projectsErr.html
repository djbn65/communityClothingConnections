Projects

Current Projects

How we are currently active in the community

No Family Left Cold

Everyone deserves to have a happy christmas, and we strive to give that opportunity to every person in our community who is in need.

Community Education

By educating the community on various topics, we are improiving the quality the community as a whole. We want to give every possible chance to improve our community members.

Community Services

We want to keep our community in a well-kept state to keep community members engaged now and draw in more for its future.

Improving Student Education

Our students are the future, so it makes sense to help improve their education as much as possible.

Charity Donations and Support

We support other charities that have similar goals to us. We want to help them get their feet on the ground and improve the community with us.

Annual Senior Christmas Special

Our senior citizens are the embodiment of our community's past and success, so we like to show support for them and give back to them.

Past Projects

Our past contributions, completed with your help

5K for Clothes

What better way to bring a community together than by running a 5K! The entry fee is a simple clothing donation of any kind.

Backpack for Success

No student should go without a backpack at school. We support our students in as many ways as possible, and this event was a big success.

Suit Up

No matter the venue or upbringing, everyone deserves to have access to professional clothing. This is exactly what this project is designed for.
