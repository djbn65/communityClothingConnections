Our Business

Mission

The Message That Got Us Started

We strive to bring communities closer together by fostering in our donors a greater connection to those in need in their community. Every event we hold and donation we receive is driven towards more connected communities as our donors help rid the less fortunate of their worries of an essential thing like clothing.

Vision

What We Aspire To Be

Our vision, ultimately, is to create communities in which no one has to worry about their essential needs for clothing. On top of this, we hope our presence and influence fosters in our community members the values we uphold, so our ideas and sense of charity become a second nature to everyone who was, and will be, a part of our program.

Values

The Practices We Hold Closest In Our Business

Volunteerism

As a business that thrives on volunteerism, we could not function without our generous volunteers. It is a strong value that we hold close in our business, and we would not be as successful as we are without it.

Charity

Our entire organization is based on charity and giving back to the community. We would not be what we are without our generous organizational members, volunteers, and charity incorporated into Community Clothing Connections.

Giving

We strive to give as much back to the community as we can, whether that be buying more clothes to donate, improving local buildings, cleaning up the city, or having events with locals.

Community

Community is foundational in our business. Without community, none of what we do would be possible. It's such a big part of our business that the word is even in our name. It's who we are.

Honesty

Honesty produces a high degree of integrity within our business. We value people who uphold our mission and are honest in all their practices. With these kinds of people, our business runs as fluidly as possible.

Stewardship

We believe it is our duty to serve and manage the well-being of our community. We are a community servicing organization, and without this value, we would be doing a disservice to its members.
