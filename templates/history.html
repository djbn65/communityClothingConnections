A Brief History Of Our Business

Our Humble Beginnings

JAN. 07, 2008

It all started here, 10 long and successful years ago with two people who ultimately wanted to help out in their community. The business grew with time and blossomed into what it is today.

Our First Event

FEB. 15, 2008

Our first event in which we got the community involved in our idea feels just like yesterday, and every event since then still feels like the first because of our loyal and supportive team.

Our First Milestone in Donations

DEC. 20, 2009

We would have never expected to receive the vast amount of donations we did this early in the business. It just goes to show that a community can do great things once it is brought together under a purpose.

Our Business Expansion

AUG. 01, 2013

To compensate for the vast amount of help and donations we were receving, we had to expand our business. We added a multipurpose room to help raise more money, and expanded the store area in our main building.

Our First Milestone in Total Volunteers

MAY. 18, 2016

When we broke 500 volunteers, we were ectastic and extremely proud with the work of the community. This was a milestone for the community itself as much as it was one for us. With a cause, a group of people can do almost anything.
