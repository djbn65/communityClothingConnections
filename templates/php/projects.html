No Family Left Cold

  • Mission: No one enjoys being cold. Sadly, when the weather turns south many people are left without any warm coats, winter boots, or even heavy blankets to bundle up with. One of our many missions is to erase this sad reality.
  • How You Can Help: Gather up any used winter clothing that is in acceptable condition and drop it off at any of our locations. Every scarf, hat, or pair of mittens brings us one step closer to a warm winter for all.

Community Education

  • Mission: We offer classes ranging over a variety of topics covering pressing issues like identifying poverty and how to approach it, as well as classes for the less fortunate like how to ace an interview.
  • How You Can Help: Drop in at our building during hours to see a full list of classes and sign up for them.

Community Services

  • Mission: While our main focus is definitely clothing necessities, Community Clothing Connections also serves as an organizer for public service events. We routinely act as a rendezvous for group projects like roadside cleanup and other volunteer groups.
  • How You Can Help: Swing by any time and we can help plug you in with a group that is suited to your skills and volunteering desires.

Improving Student Education

  • Mission: We want the students in our community to have the best education possible. Because of this, we are constantly donating time and money to improve student education in many different areas to allow students to reach their highest potential.
  • How You Can Help: Donations are always appreciated, and we could always use extra help when we host any sort of event for local students. Just talk to a worker at our main store, call, or email us for additional information.

Charity Donations and Support

  • Mission: We remember how hard it was for us to start our business, and we don’t want that same thing happening to other charities and businesses like ours. Because of this, we are constantly helping out local charities by supporting their events through voluntary work, and we also give monetary support from time to time as well.
  • How You Can Help: We always have an up-to-date list of all events going on in the community inside our main building if you would ever like to come pick one up!

Annual Senior Christmas Special

  • Mission: In our organization, and even more in our community, we like to give as much back to our elders as we can. They have been around the longest and have affected our community the most, so we like to give back to them every Christmas. We also just love seeing the smiles on their faces when they come in to the dinner and have such a good time.
  • How You Can Help: If you have anything you would like to donate, we will accept donations all year round. Also, if you would like to volunteer your help at the event, it would be greatly appreciated.

5K for Clothes

  • Mission: The 5K for Clothes is one of our favorite events. Every year the community comes together in a friendly competition to support those in need. With the help of donations and an entry fee, this event is one of our most effective in providing for the community.
  • How You Can Help: Pencil it into your calendar, we look forward to seeing you there!

Backpack for Success

  • Mission: Backpacks can be expensive, but they are very necessary for today’s student to succeed. We had a backpack drive last year and provided over 150 backpacks to students who otherwise would have gone without..
  • How You Can Help: We still accept backpacks as well as other school supplies/clothes. Simply drop them off at any of our four drop-off locations and we’ll take care of the rest.

Suit Up

  • Mission: We strive to help the less fortunate better their situation and providing formal dress clothing is an important part of that. We accept formal dresses, suits, or other dress clothing in reasonable condition to help those who can’t afford such items.
  • How You Can Help: Drop off any used dress clothing or even donate new items at any of our drop-off locations.
